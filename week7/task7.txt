
Learn how to divide a webpage into multiple sections using the <frameset> and <frame> elements.

    
    Instructions:

    Create three files:

    index.html

    menu.html

    content.html

    In index.html,

    Use a <frameset> to split the page into two columns:
        the left side 30% width and the right side 70%.

        The left frame should display menu.html.

        The right frame should display content.html.

        In menu.html,

        Create a simple list of links:
        Home, About, Contact

        Each link should open in the right frame (use target="main").

        In content.html,

        Write a short welcome message and an image.
